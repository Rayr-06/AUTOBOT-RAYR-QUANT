## 📊 Strategy Details

### Entry Criteria (Score ≥ 72)
- ✅ Bullish trend (EMAs aligned: 9 > 21 > 50 > 200)
- ✅ Strong momentum (RSI 55-65, MACD bullish)
- ✅ Volume surge (2x average)
- ✅ Bullish supertrend
- ✅ Clear structure (higher highs & lows)
- ✅ Volatility contraction (VCP pattern)
- ✅ Near key support
- ✅ Favorable risk/reward (3:1+)

### Exit Criteria (Score < 50)
- ❌ Momentum weakening
- ❌ Multiple factors misaligned
- ❌ Risk/reward deteriorating
