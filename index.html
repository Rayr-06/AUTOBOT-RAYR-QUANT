📡 Connect Your Bot
This dashboard needs to connect to your bot running on your computer. Follow the steps below to set up the connection.
1

Start Your Bot

On your computer, navigate to your bot folder and run:

START_ALL.bat

This starts both the trading bot and the API server

2

Get Your Computer's IP

Open Command Prompt (CMD) and type:

ipconfig

Look for "IPv4 Address" (example: 192.168.1.100)

3

Access Dashboard

On your phone or any device, open:

http://YOUR_IP:8000

Replace YOUR_IP with the IP from step 2
Example: http://192.168.1.100:8000
